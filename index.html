Overview

The Youth Dividend is a research-oriented online economics journal led by a team of passionate young economists. Our mission is to analyze and communicate complex economic ideas in a clear, insightful, and engaging manner. We publish well-researched articles covering a wide range of economic topics, from global economic trends to policy impacts, economic inequality, and various many more.


Our platform serves as a space for aspiring economists to explore, debate, and share perspectives on pressing economic issues. By blending academic rigor with accessible writing, we aim to bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications, fostering a deeper understanding of economics among students, professionals, and the general public.


With a dedicated editorial team, regular publications, and an active social media presence, The Youth Dividend is committed to making economics more approachable and impactful for the next generation of thinkers and decision-makers.
